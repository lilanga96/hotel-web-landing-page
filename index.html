About Us

Discover the tranquility and beauty of our forest retreat, where comfort meets nature.

Nestled in the heart of East London, Eastern Cape, Bamblymn Forest Leisure offers a serene escape amidst nature's finest landscapes. Our hotel is a haven for travelers seeking a perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and adventure. Surrounded by lush forests and tranquil greenery, Bamblymn Forest Leisure provides an idyllic retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life. ...

Bamblymn Forest Leisure was born out of a vision to create a sanctuary where guests can reconnect with nature while enjoying world-class amenities. Our commitment to excellence and passion for hospitality are reflected in every aspect of our service, ensuring that your stay with us is nothing short of extraordinary.
